A little about me.

I'm a data-driven professional with a strong background in operations, compliance, and risk management — building the systems, tools, and reporting infrastructure that keep complex organizations running cleanly.

With over a decade of cross-industry experience, I specialize in turning scattered, siloed data into clear and actionable insight. Currently based at Greystar's corporate office in Seattle, I work across a multi-state portfolio where data integrity and operational precision are non-negotiable.

I'm completing my B.S. in Data Analytics at WSU and working toward a long-term career in data and analytics engineering, with a focus on making complex data actually useful for the people who need it.

I believe strongly in growth at every level — both as a mentor and a mentee. I currently serve as a RISE Program Mentor at Greystar and am an active participant in the WMFHA Emerging Leaders Program. Good mentorship, in both directions, is one of the most underrated tools in any workplace.
